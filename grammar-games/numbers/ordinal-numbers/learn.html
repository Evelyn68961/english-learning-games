Ordinal Numbers

Learn the order of things in English!

🤔 What are Ordinal Numbers?

Cardinal numbers tell us how many (1, 2, 3...).
Ordinal numbers tell us the order or position (1st, 2nd, 3rd...).

基數告訴我們「多少」，序數告訴我們「第幾」！

⚡ The Big Three — Irregular! (不規則)

The first three ordinal numbers are special. They don't follow any rule — you need to memorize them!

前三個序數是特別的，需要直接記住！

Memorize these!
1 → first (1st)   2 → second (2nd)   3 → third (3rd)

📏 Regular Rule: Add -th (4th–10th)

Most numbers just add -th to become ordinal. But watch out for special spelling changes!

大部分的數字只要加上 -th 就變成序數，但有些拼字會改變！

Basic Rule
number + -th
Number Ordinal Note
4 (four)→fourth (4th)regular
5 (five)→fifth (5th)ve → fth ⚠️
6 (six)→sixth (6th)regular
7 (seven)→seventh (7th)regular
8 (eight)→eighth (8th)no double t ⚠️
9 (nine)→ninth (9th)drop the e ⚠️
10 (ten)→tenth (10th)regular
⚠️ Watch the spelling!
• five → fifth (not fiveth)
• eight → eighth (not eightth)
• nine → ninth (not nineth)

🔟 The Teens: 11th–19th

All teen numbers add -th. Remember the special spelling for twelfth!

所有十幾的數字都加 -th。注意 twelfth 的特殊拼法！

Number Ordinal Note
11 (eleven)→eleventh (11th)regular
12 (twelve)→twelfth (12th)ve → fth ⚠️
13 (thirteen)→thirteenth (13th)regular
14 (fourteen)→fourteenth (14th)regular
15 (fifteen)→fifteenth (15th)regular
16–19→+ thregular

✨ The Tens: 20th–90th

For numbers ending in -y, change the y to ie and add -th.

以 -y 結尾的數字，把 y 改成 ie 再加 -th！

Tens Rule
-y → -ieth
Number Ordinal
20 (twenty)→twentieth (20th)
30 (thirty)→thirtieth (30th)
40 (forty)→fortieth (40th)
50 (fifty)→fiftieth (50th)
60–90→same pattern: -ieth

🔗 Compound Numbers: 21st–99th

For compound numbers, only the last word changes. The tens part stays the same!

複合數字中，只有最後一個字變成序數，前面的十位數不變！

Compound Rule
tens (stay the same) + ordinal of ones
Number Ordinal
21→twenty-first (21st)
32→thirty-second (32nd)
43→forty-third (43rd)
55→fifty-fifth (55th)
68→sixty-eighth (68th)
99→ninety-ninth (99th)

💯 One Hundredth (100th)

100 = one hundred → 100th = one hundredth

一百 → 第一百

Just add -th!
hundred → hundredth (100th)

🎒 When Do We Use Ordinal Numbers? (Student Life!)

Ordinal numbers are everywhere in school! Here are common ways to use them:

  • 📅
    Dates:
    My birthday is on March 3rd.
    我的生日在三月三日。
  • 🏢
    Floors & Classrooms:
    Our classroom is on the 4th floor.
    我們的教室在四樓。
  • 🏆
    Rankings & Competitions:
    She got 2nd place in the race.
    她在賽跑中得到第二名。
  • 📖
    Order & Sequence:
    Open your book to the 5th page.
    翻開課本到第五頁。
  • 🎂
    Celebrations:
    This is our school's 50th anniversary.
    這是我們學校的五十週年。
  • ❓
    Questions:
    Is this your first time here?
    這是你第一次來嗎？
  • 🚫
    Negative Sentences:
    It is not the first time I've been late.
    這不是我第一次遲到。
